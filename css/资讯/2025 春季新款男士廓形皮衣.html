2025 Spring New Arrival: Unisex Oversized Leather Jacket for Men 2025 春季新款男士廓形皮衣

January 15, 2026 2026-01-15

Blending Korean streetwear aesthetics with the rugged edge of classic moto styling, this leather jacket is the cornerstone piece for crafting effortlessly cool, urban looks. Designed for springtime wind resistance and layered winter versatility, it’s a unisex essential that transitions seamlessly across seasons and styles. 融合韩系潮流与机车夹克的硬朗气质，这款皮衣是打造街头酷感造型的核心单品，兼具春季防风与秋冬叠穿实用性，男女同款，轻松适配多种风格。

I. Fabric & Texture 一、面料质感

• Premium Faux or Genuine Leather: Features a refined pebbled (lychee) grain surface—soft yet resilient, with a sophisticated matte finish.Highly resistant to wrinkles, abrasions, and everyday scuffs, making it low-maintenance and durable for daily wear. • 优质仿皮 / 真皮面料：表面呈现细腻荔枝纹肌理，触感柔韧且自带哑光高级感，抗皱耐磨，日常穿着不易留下划痕，打理便捷。

• The fabric has natural “body” and structure, providing excellent support for the oversized, dropped-shoulder silhouette—maintaining its sharp, three-dimensional shape even after extended use. • 廓形挺括度佳：面料自带筋骨感，能支撑起宽松落肩的版型，不易软塌，久穿依然保持立体轮廓。

II. Craftsmanship & Details 二、工艺细节

• Smooth Metal Zipper: Full-front heavy-duty metal zipper ensures effortless, snag-free operation. The stand-up or fold-down collar offers styling flexibility—from edgy moto to relaxed casual. • 利落拉链设计：采用顺滑耐用的金属拉链，拉合顺畅不卡顿，正面全开襟设计穿脱便捷，领口可立可翻，适配不同造型需求。

• Adjustable Cuff Tabs: Snap-button straps at the cuffs allow personalized fit around the wrist, enhancing wind protection while adding subtle hardware detailing that elevates the overall look. • 精致袖口调节：袖口配备按扣袢带设计，可根据手腕粗细调节松紧，防风保暖的同时提升整体造型细节感。

III. Design & Styling Versatility 三、设计与适配

• Korean-Inspired Oversized Cut: Relaxed through the shoulders and body, this silhouette flatters all body types with intentional ease—creating that coveted laid-back, street-savvy vibe. • 韩系廓形剪裁：宽松落肩版型不挑身材，对各种体型都有很好的包容度，打造慵懒随性的街头氛围感。

• Versatile Styling Appeal:Effortlessly pairs with T-shirts or hoodies for a laid-back, everyday look, or layers over turtlenecks and button-down shirts to evoke a retro moto aesthetic. A true wardrobe staple that blends seamlessly into countless outfits—combining timeless attitude with everyday wearability. • 风格百搭属性：可搭配 T 恤、卫衣打造休闲日常风，也能内搭高领毛衣、衬衫营造复古机车感，是衣橱里的百搭型格单品。
